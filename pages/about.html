About Us


Sew'n'Beads is a unique sewing business which applies knowledge and experience gained over the last 25 years. We have a qualified tutor who has studied at London College of Fashion teaching classes from dressmaking through to bags, curtain making, patchwork and quilting and almost everything else to do with sewing.

We are a small but welcoming and passionate company dedicating all of our resources to teaching anybody over the age of seven.
